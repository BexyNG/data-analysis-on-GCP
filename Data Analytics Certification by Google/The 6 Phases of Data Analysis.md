# The 6 Phases of Data Analysis

**Ask** - Define the problem and understand stakeholder expectations by asking effective questions

**Prepare** - Collect and store relevant data while ensuring it meets quality and integrity standards

**Process** - Clean and transform data by removing errors, inconsistencies, and irrelevant information

**Analyze** - Examine data to identify patterns, relationships, and insights that answer your questions

**Share** - Communicate findings through visualizations, reports, and presentations to stakeholders

**Act** - Use insights to make data-driven decisions and implement solutions to solve the original problem
